The game "Snake"

Score: 0

Instructions for the game

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard (left, right, up, down) to control the movement of the snake. Press the corresponding arrow key to change the direction of the snake's movement.
  • The goal of the game is to collect the food that appears on the game field. When the snake's head collides with the food, the snake will grow longer in length.
